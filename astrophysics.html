COOL-LAMPS VIII
Known Wide-separation Lensed Quasars
COOL-LAMPS VIII
We studied six rare, wide-separation lensed quasars and their host galaxies. By combining multiple measurement techniques, we find these host galaxies include both star-forming and quiescent types. There is no clear evidence for evolution in the black hole-to-stellar mass ratio since early cosmic times.

COOL-LAMPS VII
Quantifying Strong-lens Scaling Relations
COOL-LAMPS VII
We measured the total mass within the Einstein radius for 177 cluster strong gravitational lenses, using photometry and redshifts from surveys. Mass, luminosity, and central galaxy redshift are strongly correlated. The mass-stellar mass relation is especially tight.
